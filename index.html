What's Héritage ?

Héritage is a french start-up, created by Clement Fromonteil, whose goal is to commercialize and make accessible foreign alcohols or alcohols that have simply disappeared.

An example : Hydromel

Hydromel is a mostly-european honey alcohol. It was drunk during the Middle-Ages and it is terribly good. Today, just walk around the shelves of a supermarket and see how many bottles you can see... So ? 0 !

The Best-Sellers

Palm Wine

Palm sap, commonly known as "palm wine", is an alcoholic beverage obtained by the natural fermentation of palm sap. It is a traditional drink in most tropical regions, and is widespread in regions near the Sahara and in sub-Saharan Africa.

Baijiu

Baijiu is China's national drink, and as a matter of fact the most consumed alcoholic drink in the world with 10 billion litres consumed per year. This sorghum alcohol is said to be thousands of years old.
